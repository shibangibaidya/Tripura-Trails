Neermahal

Location: Melaghar, Tripura

Coordinates: 23.5077° N, 91.2872° E

Historical Significance: Neermahal, meaning "Water Palace," is a former royal palace built by King Bir Bikram Kishore Manikya Bahadur in the middle of Rudrasagar Lake. It blends Hindu and Mughal architectural styles.

Open: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Entry Fee: ₹ 30 / person

Matabari Temple

Location: Udaipur, Tripura

Coordinates: 23.5293° N, 91.4996° E

Historical Significance: Matabari Temple, also known as Tripura Sundari Temple, is one of the 51 Shakti Peethas dedicated to Goddess Kali. It attracts pilgrims from all over India, especially during the Diwali festival.

Open: 6:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Sepahijala Wildlife Sanctuary

Location: Bishalgarh, Tripura

Coordinates: 23.6638° N, 91.3238° E

Known for: Sepahijala Wildlife Sanctuary is home to a variety of flora and fauna, including clouded leopards, monkeys, and a rich variety of birds. It also houses a botanical garden, zoo, and lake for boating.
